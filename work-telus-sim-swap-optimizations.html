SIM Swap Optimizations

Improving E2E journey for SIM Swap.

Desktop and mobile view of the re-designed SIM Swap

Context

When you purchase a SIM card on Telus.com, you have to activate it using MyTelus. Analytics showed that customers were falling out on Step 1 of the flow. After some investigation and user testing, we found out that customers were not able to find the SIM card number to start. Additionally, the cognitive load on step 1 was high due to the amount of content.

Challenge

Optimize the end-to-end experience so customers are able to change their SIM card(s).

My role

Designer, leading the project discovery, design concepts, prototyping, user testing, and gathering feedback from various stakeholders. I worked alongside with product owners, engineers, and business customer support team.

Analytics before re-design: Majority of the customers fall out at Step 1

Success metrics

  • Increased self-serve conversions.
  • Improved web rating and VOC (voice of customer).
  • Reduce calls, and reduce customer frustration.
  • Improved user experience.

    • Problems with the current design: cognitive overload
