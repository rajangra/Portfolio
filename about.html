About Rajan

Who is Rajan?

A versatile product designer with strong expertise in design and research. I value honest communication, highly-collaborative environments, with individuals that are most importantly - not afraid to have fun in the process. I believe in creating products that are a delight to use, and impactful to both the user and the business. With 5+ years of experience in product design, research, motion, visual, and coding, I bring a unique skillset that great teams love.

I am also a licensed drone pilot. Shot on a DJI drone: beautiful city of Vancouver.

How did I get here?

Growing up, I was often the person helping others with technology. I knew I wanted to do something that allowed me to use this skill. After studying Interaction design, I explored various disciplines. I even tried coding! Product Design allows me to use my multidisciplinary skills, and learn something new every day. Now, I can help not only the people around me, but millions of people across the world.
