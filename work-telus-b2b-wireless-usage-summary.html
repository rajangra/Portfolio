Wireless Usage Summary

Providing a holistic view of an account's data usage.

Mobile and desktop view of the re-designed usage summary

Context

Today, when a TELUS business customer wants to find out who is overusing data, they need to check each subscriber individually. Many business customers have thousands of subscribers. This makes the experience time consuming, frustrating, and impossible to find out who is causing the data overages.

In fact, some corporate customers have had over $200k in overages within a month.

Challenge

Create a holistic usage view that allows business customers to view and manage their subscribers.

My role

Designer, leading the project discovery, design concepts, prototyping, user testing, and gathering feedback from various stakeholders. I worked alongside with product owners, engineers, and business customer support team.
